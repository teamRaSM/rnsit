COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING
Dr. G. T. Raju
Dean of Engg, Professor and Head

The department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 2001 right from the inception of the Institution with an objective of imparting quality technical education in IT field. The department is currently headed by Dr. G T Raju who has more than 25 years of experience in the field. The department offers BE and M.Tech degrees in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) with an intake 120 UG students and 24 PG students respectively. The department has a VTU recognized R&D centre and currently 18 research scholars are pursuing their research work leading to PhD/MSc (Engg) degrees. The department has state-of-the-art laboratories, library facility for staff &students and boasts of well trained, committed and experienced teaching faculty with an average teaching experience of 10.04 years, who emphasize the theoretical and practical applications of computation and computer systems to the needs of society.

The department is striving hard to make the unique spirit of RNSIT, an Institution with a difference by promoting excellence in Teaching, Research and Services covering the fundamentals as well as applied aspects of Computer Science and IT.

The fast changing scenario in Computer Science and Engineering necessitates the department to actively extend its focus and activities in

  1. Research and Development
  2. Consultancy in IT related areas and Sponsored projects apart from its excellent conventional UG and PG teaching.

Being a forerunner in the field of technology, the department envisages evolving as a center of excellence to adapt itself to the rapid advancements in various specialized fields. Computer Science and Engineering continues to be the driving force of the nation's economy in the fields of science and technology. We in the CSE department, at RNSIT, are very excited to be part of this information revolution.

Vision

Better Computer Professsionals for a Real World.

Mission

  1. To promote an intellectual and ethical environment in which the strengths and skills will flourish
  2. To impart solid foundations and applied aspects in both Computer Science theory and Programming practices
  3. To educate and prepare the graduates, highly sought after and productive, and well-respected for their work culture
  4. To Provide training and encouraging R&D and Consultancy Services in frontier areas of Computer Science with Global outlook

Objectives/Goals

Short Range

  1. To build and use modern aids for effective teaching
  2. To modernize the Laboratories with state-of-the-art software, hardware, Interfacing and networking components
  3. To ensure all-round development of students through conduction of self-sustained training programs
  4. To organize and conduct short-term courses/workshops/FDPs/seminars on recent trends in IT at National and International levels on regular basis

Long Range

  1. To become part of the Industry and Research organizations at National and International levels
    1. To promote Industry-Institute Interactions through projects, consultancy and executive development programs
    2. To promote research and development activities in the frontier areas of Computer Science and engineering
  2. To enter into understanding with globally renowned Universities
  3. To develop Centers of Excellence in core areas
    1. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
    2. Internet of Things, Digital/Device Mesh, User Experience
  4. To strengthen Entrepreneurship Development Cell
    1. To provide necessary strengths for enabling the students to innovate and become entrepreneurs
