COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

Dr. G. T. Raju

Dean of Engg, Professor and Head

The department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 2001 right from the inception of the Institution with an objective of imparting quality technical education in IT field. The department is currently headed by Dr. G T Raju who has more than 22 years of experience in the field. The department offers BE and M.Tech degrees in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) with an intake 120 UG students and 24 PG students respectively. The department has a VTU recognized R&D centre and currently 18 research scholars are pursuing their research work leading to PhD/MSc (Engg) degrees. The department has state-of-the-art laboratories, library facility for staff & students and boasts of well trained, committed and experienced teaching faculty with an average teaching experience of 10.04 years, who emphasize the theoretical and practical applications of computation and computer systems to the needs of society.

The department is striving hard to make the unique spirit of RNSIT, an Institution with a difference by promoting excellence in Teaching, Research and Services covering the fundamentals as well as applied aspects of Computer Science and IT.

The fast changing scenario in Computer Science and Engineering necessitates the department to actively extend its focus and activities in

  1. Research and Development
  2. Consultancy in IT related areas and Sponsored projects apart from its excellent conventional UG and PG teaching.

Being a forerunner in the field of technology, the department envisages evolving as a center of excellence to adapt itself to the rapid advancements in various specialized fields. Computer Science and Engineering continues to be the driving force of the nation's economy in the fields of science and technology. We in the CSE department, at RNSIT, are very excited to be part of this information revolution.

Laboratory

The department has well equipped laboratory with sophisticated instruments. The practical classes are assisted by the well experienced lab instructor Mrs. Bhagyalakshmi. With all these strengths, the department has achieved 100% results in the past years. The department also encourages students updating their knowledge by involving them in seminars, invited talks and such activities

The Department of Physics conducted a National Conference on Phosphors and their Applications (NCPA 2010) during November 15-16, 2010. The conference had 111 participants from various parts of the country and 65 papers were presented in the conference. In the year 2011 the department conducted two events-

  1. Intercollegiate Physics Fest PHOTON 2011 on May 5th 2011
  2. National Seminar on Nuclear Technology for the Growth of the Nation on August 22, 2011.
