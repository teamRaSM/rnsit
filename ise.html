INFORMATION SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

Dr. C. Pandurangappa

Professor & Head

The Department of Information Science and Engineering was established in the year 2001 with an intake of 40 and the intake has been increased to 60 in the year 2002. Now in the year 2012 the intake has increased to 120. The Department also runs a Post-Graduate program in Computer Network & Engineering with an intake of 18.

The Department is Headed by Dr. M V Sudhamani who has more than 25 years of Teaching experience and specialization in image processing, Advanced Algorithms & Databases.

Currently guiding 5 candidates for Ph.D under VTU & carrying out two research projects sponsored by AICTE & VTU.

She is recipient of Bharath Jyothi Award by IIFS, New Delhi.

The department has well equipped laboratory with sophisticated instruments. The practical classes are assisted by the well experienced lab instructor Mrs. Bhagyalakshmi. With all these strengths, the department has achieved 100% results in the past years. The department also encourages students updating their knowledge by involving them in seminars, invited talks and such activities

The Department of Physics conducted a National Conference on Phosphors and their Applications (NCPA 2010) during November 15-16, 2010. The conference had 111 participants from various parts of the country and 65 papers were presented in the conference. In the year 2011 the department conducted two events-

  1. Intercollegiate Physics Fest PHOTON 2011 on May 5th 2011
  2. National Seminar on Nuclear Technology for the Growth of the Nation on August 22, 2011.

Faculty

The Department currently comprises of 15 highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are committed to give to the best. The staff members are determined to bring ingenuity and innovative methods in teaching. Faculty research interest spans a wide range : image processing & feature extraction, Advanced Algorithms, Computer Graphics, Advanced DBMS, Information Retrieval,Networks, etc.,

The Department also has experienced technical staff who help the students in technical aspects. The Department has State-of-Art laboratories, library facility for Staff & Students. The Department is conducting activities like orientation program, guest lectures, seminars / workshops for the purpose to keep in pace with the latest trends in software industry.

The Department has bagged 4 ranks in VTU Examinations.

Short Range Goals

  1. Enhance the academic performance of the faculty and students on continual basis.
  2. Organize experts lectures, seminars etc., to enhance the knowledge.
  3. Boost up the technical knowledge of the students be providing opportunities to actively involve in paper presentation, hobby projects, technical contest etc.
  4. Tune up the department to meet the increasing demands of software industry.

Long Range Goals

  1. To Lay a strong foundation to promote R & D activities.
  2. To set up a base for the people from industries and other institutions to impart quality training programme.

Vision

Fostering winning professionals of Strong informative potential.

Mission

Imparting high quality education in the area of Information Science so as to graduate the students with good fundamentals, "Information System Integration", "Software Creation" capability & suitably train them to thrive in Industries, higher schools of learning & R & D centers with a comprehensive perspective.
