CIVIL ENGINEERING
Dr.Pratap Kumar MT
Professor & Head

The Department of Civil Engineering is proud to offer Bachelor of Engineering course in Civil Engineering under VTU from 2014-15 with an initial instake of 60 students, under the guidance of the great visionary in Civil Engingeering constructions, Dr. R N Shetty.

Civil Engineering Department of RNS Institute of Technology, Bangalore, aims to be a centre of excellence in technical education and innovative applications, providing excellent professionals with integrity, besides being useful to all the stakeholders.

The department aims to be the best-rated department in college and in the university disseminating globally acceptable education, effective industrial consultancy and relevant research output.







The objectives are as follows:
  1. Provide high quality civil engineering education to the graduate program to cope up with national / international standards
  2. Prepare graduates for professionals career and doctoral research
  3. Be a leading consultancy provider for the development of the society
  4. Promote excellence in teaching and research

Vision

To provide Valuable resources for industry and society through excellence in technical education and research by developing Professional Civil Engineers, To offer Continuing Education with interaction with Industry with emphasis on R&D.

Mission

  1. Development of human intellectual capability to its fullest potential in the field of Civil Engineering
  2. Recognizing the need for and have the broadest context of technological change in Civil Engineering
  3. Generate new knowledge by engaging in cutting-edge research projects.
