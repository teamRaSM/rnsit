ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

Dr. Vipula Singh

Professor & Head

The department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was started in 2001 and is currently running B.E, M.Tech and Research programs. The department is headed by Dr. Vipula Singh, who has around 18 years of experience in teaching and research. She has obtained her doctorate from Guru Gobind Singh Indra Prastha University New Delhi in the area of image processing. She has published more than 21 papers in international / national Journals and Conferences. She is currently guiding 2 students for Ph.D and 1 student for MS. She is the author a book on image processing.

In 2012-13, UG intake has increased from 120 to 180 and PG intake has increased from 18 to 24. The department has 30 faculty members, with 4 professors and the rest with a PG degree. Two professors are doctorates with specialization in Wireless communication and image processing. The Department has VTU recognized R & D centre and 13 faculty members are pursuing Ph.D in diverse areas of ECE, such as, Digital Electronics, VLSI, Biomedical Engineering, Power Electronics, Image Processing and Communication. The department has well equipped laboratory with sophisticated instruments. The practical classes are assisted by the well experienced lab instructor Mrs. Bhagyalakshmi. With all these strengths, the department has achieved 100% results in the past years. The department also encourages students updating their knowledge by involving them in seminars, invited talks and such activities

The Department of Physics conducted a National Conference on Phosphors and their Applications (NCPA 2010) during November 15-16, 2010. The conference had 111 participants from various parts of the country and 65 papers were presented in the conference. In the year 2011 the department conducted two events-

  1. Intercollegiate Physics Fest PHOTON 2011 on May 5th 2011
  2. National Seminar on Nuclear Technology for the Growth of the Nation on August 22, 2011.

Vision

Conquering technical frontiers in the field of Electronics and Communication.

Mission

  1. To achieve and foster excellence in core Electronics and Communication with focus on Hardware, Software and Design
  2. To pursue research, development and consultancy to achieve self sustenance
  3. To create benchmark standards in E&C by active involvement of all stake holders.

Other Objectives

Short Range
  1. Train students to meet industry challenges
  2. Improve infrastructure for R&D
  3. Strengthen the number of Doctorates

Long Range
  1. Create Center of Excellence in Embedded systems , VLSI & Communication
  2. Foreign University collaboration
  3. Solution providers to industry
