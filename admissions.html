ADMISSIONS

Common Entereance Test (CET)
TOP MOST ENGINEERING COLLEGES IN KARNATAKA

  1. The Government of Karnataka conducts Common Entrance Test (CET) for admission into various professional courses. The CET is generally conducted during Feb/March and the notification will appear in all leading newspapers. RNSIT is being in the list of top most engineering colleges in karnataka.
  2. A merit list will be prepared based on the combined performance in PUC/XII std. and CET.
  3. Seat matrix are announced indicating seats available in different colleges in different branches based on the sanctioned intake.
  4. Admissions are made according to merit, subject to reservations to various categories.
  5. Students called for counseling on a particular day will be announced on previous day in leading newspapers.
  6. On the day of counseling, when his/her turn comes, he/she can choose the college and branch based on the vacancy position displayed online.
  7. Students also get the opportunity to change the branch/college only once before the end of admission and this is final.


    8. Change of branch: As per the VTU regulations, change of branch is permitted only at the beginning of 3rd semester provided vacancy exists. The change of branch is done according to prescribed regulations and is subject to approval by Director of Technical Education and the University. Providing the better education and maintaining the reputation has made RNSIT to be in the list of one of the top most engineering colleges in karnataka.
